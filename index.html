What Is Toss?

Toss is a personal styling application that uses your existing wardrobe to curate outfits for you based on event type, weather and mood.

Personalized Home Screen

Everytime you open the app, it gives you the weather for your location and a series of events to pick for that day.

Your 'Tossed' Outfit

The app then curates multiple perfect outfits for your day, including all of the details of that outfit.

Save It To Your Calendar

Once you choose what you're wearing, it is then automatically uploaded to your outfit calendar.

Why Toss?

Toss was created for the fashion conscious individual that
wants the luxury of a personal stylist with our the price tag.

We have created a more sustainable and efficient use of
